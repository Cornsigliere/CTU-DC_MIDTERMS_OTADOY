About US

        Cebu Technological University - Danao Campus is a branch of the prominent Cebu Technological University system, located in Danao City, Cebu, Philippines. It serves as an educational hub offering a diverse range of academic programs, including undergraduate and graduate degrees, diploma courses, and technical vocational programs. Committed to addressing the region's skill requirements, the campus provides technical and vocational training, equipping students with practical skills for various industries. Additionally, CTU - Danao Campus is engaged in research and development activities, collaborates with local industries and communities, and boasts a range of facilities, such as classrooms, laboratories, and libraries. It plays a vital role in the community's growth, impacting the local economy and contributing to knowledge exchange and development. Accredited by relevant education authorities in the Philippines, the campus ensures that its programs and degrees meet national standards, promoting quality education and skill development.
