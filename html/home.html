Spartans Win Volleyball.

Team Captain Christa Jean Espiga credited the team's triumph to members' skills and determination, overcoming formidable challenges.

With great power, strong mind, coordination, and unwavering determination, the College of Technology (COT) Spartans secured the championship with two consecutive game victories against the College of Engineering (COE) Samurai: 25-23, 25-14 (Game 1) 25-23, and 25-23 (Game 2).
